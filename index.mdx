## Mitigate Churn
Engage users at the critical moment of subscription cancellation. By presenting a valuable alternative path to your premium features, Encore gives you a powerful new tool to retain users who would otherwise be lost, dramatically increasing customer lifetime value.

## Drive Incremental Revenue
Turn paywall declines into a new revenue stream. For the vast majority of users who are not yet ready to subscribe, Encore provides a way to monetize their engagement by connecting them with relevant offers from premium brands.

## Enhance the User Experience
Provide a valuable, user-respectful alternative that feels like a feature, not an ad. Our native, polished UI ensures the experience is seamless, giving users a sense of agency and a new way to access the features they want.
